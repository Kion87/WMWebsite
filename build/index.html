What's the purpose of buy-backs

Earth Node Operators and Delegators get paid for producing blocks from the inflation rewards (29% of WMT supply) and for providing the telecom services (phone calls etc.). The ENOs will be paid a percentage of what users spend.As ENOs are paid in WMT, not fiat, this percentageis then used for the BuyBack - WM buys WMT rom the open market and distribute the tokens to the ENOs for their work. https://wmtscan.com/
